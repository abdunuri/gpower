What types of batteries do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of batteries including lithium-ion, lead-acid, and gel batteries suitable for various applications:

  • Lithium-ion Batteries: High efficiency, long lifespan (5-10 years), perfect for solar systems and EVs
  • Lead-Acid Batteries: Cost-effective, reliable performance for backup power systems
  • Gel Batteries: Maintenance-free, deep cycle capabilities for renewable energy storage

All our batteries come with warranties and are optimized for Ethiopian climate conditions.

How efficient are your solar panels?

Our solar panels feature high-efficiency monocrystalline technology with efficiency rates ranging from 19% to 22%. Key features include:

  • High temperature coefficient performance
  • Low light efficiency optimization
  • 25-year linear performance warranty
  • Resistance to dust and harsh weather conditions

What is the lifespan of your energy storage systems?

Our energy storage systems are designed for longevity:

We provide comprehensive maintenance services to extend system lifespan.
