About G-Power

Powering Ethiopia with Reliable Green Energy G-Power Manufacturing PLC was founded in 2023 with the clear goal of bringing clean, dependable, and sustainable power solutions to people and businesses across Ethiopia. We believe that reliable energy is the key to staying connected, productive, and ready for the future. From our modern headquarters at G-Power Tower in Jemo Michael, next to Anbessa Garage, we design and deliver energy systems that protect homes, businesses, and industries from power cuts and fluctuations. Our products range from solar and battery backup solutions for houses and offices to high-performance systems for data centers, telecoms, and farms. We are proud to be a sister company of Five Star Elevator Manufacturing Plc, a trusted name in the construction sector. This strong foundation allows us to combine advanced technology with proven quality, ensuring our customers always receive solutions they can count on.
